Venison fingers Recipes

Ingredients

This is venison fingers recipe

  • 1 quart oil for frying
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups Parmesan cheese
  • 1 ¼ cup Italian seasoning
  • 1 pound venison steaks, cut into strips
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup prepared mustard
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat the oil in skillet or deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: In a bowl, mix the flour, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Dip venison strips in egg, then in the flour mixture to evenly coat.
  3. Step 3: Fry coated venison strips in the hot oil until golden brown.
  4. Step 4: Melt the brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat, and mix in the mustard. Serve as a dipping sauce with venison strips.
